## What is EchoForge?

**EchoForge** is a fully offline voice changer for Android and iPhone that records your voice, applies one of 24 carefully tuned effects (or a stack of effects you build yourself), and shares the result directly to messaging apps. The whole pipeline — recording, processing, encoding the audio file — runs on your device. There are no ads, no account, no internet permission, and no tracking.

The unique part: **stackable effect chains**. You can layer pitch, reverb, echo, distortion, bitcrush and more, drag-and-drop them into the order you want, and save the result as your own custom voice. Most voice changer apps lock that level of customization behind a subscription. EchoForge keeps it free.

## How EchoForge Works

**Q: Where does the audio processing happen?**
A: Entirely on the device. EchoForge does not require an internet connection at any step.

**Q: Are my voice recordings uploaded?**
A: Never. Audio stays on the device. There is no cloud, no account, and no telemetry.

**Q: Can I really stack effects?**
A: Yes. Add pitch, reverb, echo, distortion, bitcrush, and EQ blocks, drag them into any order, and tweak each one's sliders. Save the chain to a custom preset slot.

**Q: Why is sharing to WhatsApp / Telegram / Discord so reliable?**
A: EchoForge exports real audio files with the correct format, sample rate, and metadata — not screenshots or broken attachments. The receiving app sees a normal voice note.

## FAQ

**Is EchoForge free?**
Yes. No ads, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions. Every effect, every preset, every export is free.

**Do I need an account?**
No. There is no sign-up, no email, no login.

**Does it use the internet?**
No. EchoForge does not request internet permission and does not connect to any server.

**Does it collect data?**
No. There are no analytics, no telemetry, no third-party SDKs.

**Why does it need microphone permission?**
To record your voice — that's the only purpose. The mic is not used otherwise.

**Why does it need files / media permission?**
To save the exported audio file and to let you share it.

**What audio formats does it export?**
Standard audio formats compatible with WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, and other messengers. The receiving app plays the clip without conversion.

**How long can a recording be?**
There is no fixed cap. Limit is your device storage.

**Real-time voice changing during a call?**
No. EchoForge transforms recorded clips, not live audio. This is by design — real-time voice modification during phone calls is restricted by mobile platforms.

**What devices are supported?**
Any Android phone or tablet with a microphone, and iPhones / iPads on iOS 13.0 or later.

**How do I report a bug?**
Open an issue at [github.com/Lapnito/echoforge/issues](https://github.com/Lapnito/echoforge/issues) or email tom@lapnito.cz.
